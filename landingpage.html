Fly With Maroon is a Travel Agency for those who want to explore countries with the best insights.
Don't settle for a typical holiday and trust our team of locals:

- Angela belives that there is no place like Italy as it is one of the most important sites in the long history of human civilization. It is an inevitable destination because of the raw beauty of its ancient cities, the magnificent gastronomy and, of course, la dolce vita. Did she mention it is also the best place to eat pizza, pasta and gelato?
- Ashwini is proud of India as it is known as the "Land of Diversity". Each state is different in culture, food, language, and religious traditions. From deserts to glacial mountain peaks, spotless white coasts to the evergreen tropical forests, there's a lot to explore and get dazzled!
- Vera would suggest Portugal, as it is a beautiful country with rich history and culture, stunning architecture, and breathtaking landscapes. From the historic cities of Lisbon and Porto to the sandy beaches of the Algarve and the rolling hills of the Douro Valley, there is something for everyone in Portugal. The country is also known for its delicious cuisine, made with fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, and its lively and friendly people. Whether you’re interested in history, nature, or simply soaking up the local atmosphere, Portugal is the perfect destination for a memorable vacation.
